Summarizing calls, rewriting emails, generating slide decks. Low-stakes side tasks that feel productive but don't move revenue.

Qualifying leads, publishing content that ranks, monitoring ad spend, keeping your CRM accurate. Still done manually, inconsistently, or not at all.

FAQ

Do you replace our agency or internal team?

Yes. We believe AI enables modern B2B companies to have a one-person marketing team that orchestrates specialized AI agents using business knowledge only a human can have. Initially, we work alongside the in-house team or agency, and then transfer knowledge of the new agentic system to increase their productivity.

What tools do you work with?

Typically: Notion, GA4, HubSpot/Salesforce, Google and Meta ad platforms, your CMS, and orchestration/automation platforms. We adapt to your stack and will recommend changes only when it materially improves speed or measurement.

How is this different from…
  • A traditional marketing agency? Agencies execute in one channel at a time. We build the operating system across your funnel, then hand you durable workflows your team can run without us.
  • Hiring a channel marketer? A specialist works in a silo. We infuse agents with that specialist knowledge so you can be the strategist, freed from executing.
  • Buying a marketing platform with "AI built in"? Platforms add features for everyone and customize for no one. Agents need to be built around your business, your data, and your workflows to actually produce results.
  • A rev ops consultant or fractional CMO? Consultants suggest strategy but aren't responsible for execution. They're also rarely technical enough to know what's actually possible with agents today.
What does pricing look like?

Most teams start with a First Agent so you can see real output before committing to a bigger system.

  • First Agent (4–6 weeks): $12k–$25k
  • Agent System (6–8 weeks): $25k–$55k
  • Marketing Bot Factory: $12k–$20k/month

If you've been quoted $30k+ by bigger "AI agencies," this will feel familiar — the difference is you get a working agent and a handoff your team can actually run.

What do you need from us?

Access to the systems we're connecting (analytics, CRM, ads, CMS), a clear owner on your side, and 30–60 minutes per week for review so we don't ship in a vacuum.

Will this break our existing systems?

No. We default to non-destructive changes, staged rollouts, and clear "before/after" reporting. If we need to touch something risky, we'll say so upfront.

How do we start?

We begin with a short discovery to confirm goals, data access, and a scoped plan for the first 4 weeks. Schedule a call to get started.
